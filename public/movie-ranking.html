The Best Movies according to Alex

My top 3 movies, but not neccesarily in order

Your Name

Awesome animation and incredible story with an unexpected twist that will blow your mind.

Fragmented

Amazing performance by James McAvoy that really gives you goosebumps as the story progresses.

The Conjuring

Best horror movie I've seen, not only it scares you, but the way the build suspense is a work of art.
